Weekly Answers: Common Questions
Q: What’s the best method to stop rust before painting?
A: Sand to bare metal, use rust converter, epoxy primer.
Q: Are cheap paint guns worth it?
A: Yes for DIY! Look for good atomization. Avoid ultra-budget no-names.
Q: How do I pop dents without cracking paint?
A: Use heat (not too much) and a glue puller. Patience is key.
Q: What grit do I need before primer?
A: 180 grit is ideal. 80 is too coarse unless you're filling.
Q: Can I spray base and clear with one gun?
A: Yes, clean between coats and watch your tip size.
